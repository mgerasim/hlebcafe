Type of campaigns

Promotion amplification

80% of users do not see promotions on the homepage

Traffic shaping

Diverting traffic to an intended page or product that converts well

Geolocation

Either traffic shaping or monetary campaign that is personalized to the recipient using browsing history and Google Analytic geo-location

Lead generation

A call to action which invites the user to enter an email address to receive a discount/offers/newsletter

Incentive marketing

Monetary or percentage discount used to attract visitors to make a purchase

Dynamic overlay

The text + image of the overlay changes depending on the visitors interest and user behaviour
